About Us:

3DXcel is a non-profit organization dedicated to making 3D printing and learning CAD accessible to all individuals, regardless of their background or financial means. We firmly believe that every person should have the opportunity to explore and harness the potential of 3D printing technology. Through our innovative programs and community initiatives, we aim to empower individuals, foster creativity, and drive positive change.

Our Story:

3DXcel came from a shared passion for 3D printing and a desire to bridge the gap between technology and education. A group of like-minded individuals, including engineers, designers, and educators, recognized the transformative power of 3D printing but also understood the barriers preventing many people from accessing it. With this realization, our organization was born. We started with a humble 3D printer in a small workshop and began offering free classes to teach the fundamentals of 3D printing and CAD software. As word spread and interest grew, so did our resources and impact. Through the dedication and support of our volunteers, donors, and partners, we expanded our reach and built a strong foundation for our mission.

Our Mission:

At 3DXcel, our mission is simple: to democratize 3D printing and CAD education by providing free resources, training, and access to equipment. We believe that by removing the barriers of cost and knowledge, we can unlock the creative potential within individuals and empower them to bring their ideas to life.

Our organization is committed to:

  • Providing free 3D printing and CAD classes: We offer comprehensive courses and workshops that teach the fundamentals of 3D printing and CAD software. Our experienced instructors guide participants through the entire process, from designing a model to printing a physical object.
  • Offering free access to 3D printing technology: We maintain a dedicated space equipped with state-of-the-art 3D printers, where individuals can bring their designs to life at no cost. By removing financial barriers, we ensure that anyone with a vision can turn it into a tangible reality.
  • Fostering a supportive community: We believe in the power of collaboration and knowledge-sharing. Through our community initiatives, we provide a platform for individuals to connect, exchange ideas, and learn from one another. Together, we can push the boundaries of what is possible with 3D printing.

Get Involved:

Are you a student or an individual interested in exploring the world of 3D printing and CAD? Here are some ways you can get involved with 3D Print for All:

  • Attend our free classes: Join our introductory courses to learn the basics of 3D printing and CAD software. Whether you're a complete beginner or have some experience, our classes are designed to cater to different skill levels.
  • Volunteer your expertise: If you have experience in 3D printing, CAD design, or teaching, consider volunteering your time and knowledge to help others. By becoming a volunteer instructor or mentor, you can make a significant impact on someone's journey.
  • Donate or sponsor: Help us continue providing free education and access to 3D printing technology by making a donation or sponsoring our programs. Your support enables us to expand our reach and empower more individuals in their creative pursuits.
  • Spread the word: Share our mission and programs with your friends, classmates, and community. By raising awareness about 3D Print for All, you can help us reach individuals who could benefit from our resources.
